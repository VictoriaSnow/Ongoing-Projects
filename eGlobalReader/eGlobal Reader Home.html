Read

Interactive, illustrated bilingual books


immerse children in leveled texts with exciting graphics and relevant content.

Teachers can use the books in the classroom as supplemental, fun literature to support single language or dual language learning curriculum.

Parents will find a fun way to expose children to a second language, listening to native speakers, and reading on their own.

Listen

Native speakers narrate every book


and offer word-by-word audio support with definitions in context in both languages. When read aloud, each word or word group is highlighted to help the reader follow the text. Every word is individually clickable for auditory support in the second language, when needed. These unique features allow students to read independently - and at their own pace - without help from a parent or teacher.

Play

Games reinforce comprehension


through associated activities that activate learning. The games also enable progress tracking in real time.

Track

Learning activity and progress


are captured for educators to inform each day's instruction, report on each child's progress and correlate individual literacy development with literacy targets.

Parents will also receive valuable feedback via an easy-to-read report so they can follow their child's progress and work with teachers to support their child's learning development.


eGlobalReader's growing library of bilingual books are written to support all language learners. They are appropriate for single language or dual language learning. Books feature basic vocabulary, popular themes, as well as global legends and fairytales.

The books can be read on Safari and Chrome browsers on a computer or tablet devices.

eGlobalReader books are currently available in Mandarin-English and Spanish-English. Look for more languages in the near future!
